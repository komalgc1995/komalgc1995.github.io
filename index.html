About Me

Presently working as a Lead Psychologist at Holistic Minds Pvt Ltd, I am leading the project for school children in the direction of enabling them to become aware of their emotions, dealing with them along with improving their soft skills.
Previously, I have worked with Mom's Belief (UN Zero Project Awardee for Innovative Project 2019), leading a team of psychologists, overseeing cases working on guiding parents on skill development of children diagnosed with Autism, ADHD, LD, GDD, CP, etc, along with promoting the importance of mental health in the society.
I have also served as a Counseling Psychologist for Healthy Mantra, Kanpur taking personal counselling sessions.
I did my Masters in Psychology from Banaras Hindu University, India, where I found my passion for promoting mental health and its importance in the society, which lead to the establishment of Pehel - A Beginning, a non-profit organisation working towards promoting the importance of mental well-being throughout the nation.
Overall, I have been in this field of Psychology to promote Mental Health and to help people deal with their day to day mental health challenges.
